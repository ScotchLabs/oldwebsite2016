The S'n'S Endowment

An Endowment Fund is a fund that is structured so that the principal amount remains intact while a percentage of the principal is released (or "drawn") each year it is active. The current rate at CMU is 5%, which means that if we have an active endowment of $50,000, the organization would draw $2,500 a year. For reference, at present day S'n'S production value, that is enough to put on our largest straight-play performance of the standard season! This type of fund is very useful as it encourages larger size donations, because you can trust that the money you are donating is going into the sustained future of the organization and not one, very expensive and caviar-catered Woodscotch, for example.

At present, we have $25,000 in the endowment and another $25,000 pledged which will reach the fund in a little over a year. At CMU, a minimum of $50,000 is required to start drawing from an endowment. Therefore, we have an established endowment, but we are not drawing from it yet. But we don't have to wait, you can help us bring up our principal total to the target of $50,000 today!
